Objectives

Review the objectives:

CO7: Describe the structure and functions of the integumentary system, to include the process of growth of the integument and repair following an injury.

  • MO7.1: List and describe the major functions of the integumentary system.
  • MO7.2: List the components of the integumentary system.
  • MO7.3: Describe the structure and functions of the epidermal, dermal, and hypodermal layers.
  • MO7.4: Describe the processes of growth and keratinization of the epidermis.
  • MO7.5: Explain the process of epidermal wound repair.
  • MO7.6: Describe the factors that contribute to skin color.
  • MO7.7: List the epidermal derivatives of the integument and describe their structures and functions.

CO14: Visually identify microscopic and macroscopic anatomical features of the integumentary, skeletal, muscular, and nervous systems.

  • MO14.1: Visually identify selected macroscopic and microscopic features of the integumentary system.
